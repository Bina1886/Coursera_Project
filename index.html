Coursera first Responsive Project

Cat

Mittens the cat went on an adventure to find a magical land filled with sunshine and mice, facing challenges along the way. She finally found the land and lived a happy life there for many years, making it her true home.

Dog

Max, a loyal dog, stayed by his sick owner's side and set out to find help. He encountered obstacles but persevered and found a medicine woman who saved his owner. Max was known as the bravest and most loyal dog in the village after his owner's recovery.

Sheep

Woolly the sheep went on an adventure to explore beyond his farm and encountered new sights. He eventually found a magical meadow with delicious grass and lived a happy life there for many years.
